Our Mission

Help S.A.V.E. USA and the National martial Arts League's have partnered to reduce crime across the United States with its "National Stop the Violence Campaign" which is detailed in Dexter V. Kennedy's book titled, "Taking on the NFL with Pro Teams KumiteSports.

In this effort we hope to establish the first ever point martial arts team league starting with 32 cities., along with addressing America's top 10 crime issues within each of the 32 cities. Within the website we need to also be able to collect the resources needed to offer training, workshops and seminars in those crime areas.
